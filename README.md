## üìñ Problem Statement
Finding scholarships is currently a chaotic process. Students have to check dozens of different websites, deal with complex eligibility rules, and often face language barriers. This portal solves that by aggregating scholarships and providing a smart matching system.

## ‚ú® Key Features

### üîç For Students
* **Smart Filtering:** Automatically matches scholarships based on Profile (Class, Income, GPA, Category).
* **AI Translation:** Uses **Google Gemini** to translate scholarship details into regional languages (Hindi, Tamil, etc.) instantly.
* **Application Dashboard:** Track the status of applied scholarships (Applied, Under Review, Accepted).
* **Simplified Application:** Easy "One-Click" redirection to official application forms.

### üõ† For Admins
* **Scholarship Management:** Add, edit, or delete scholarship listings.
* **Database Seeding:** Quickly populate the portal with opportunities.
